How to compile & run our code:

1. Load a Java IDE (For example Eclipse)
2. Click the 'File' tab
3. Click 'Import' from the drop-down list
4. Click 'File System'
5. Click 'Next'
6. Click 'Browse'
7. Browse to the Group2-A2 folder
8. Click the 'Source' subfolder
9. Click 'Select Folder'
10. Check the box next to the folder name if it is not checked already
11. Click 'Finish'
12. Click the 'Run' tab
13. Click 'Run Configurations' from the drop-down list
14. Double-click 'Java application' from the left-hand menu
15. Click the 'Browse' button
16. Click the 'Source' folder
17. Click the 'Search' button
18. Click the 'Screen' class
19. Click 'Apply
20. Click 'Run
